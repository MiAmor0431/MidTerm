Education

Smagulov school (2017-2023)

Smagulov School, located in Almaty, is a specialized physical and mathematical school designed for gifted children. Known for its rigorous academic curriculum, it focuses on advanced studies in mathematics, physics, and related disciplines. The school fosters a challenging and supportive environment, encouraging students to excel in their academic pursuits while also nurturing their critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

SDU University (2023-2027

SDU (Suleyman Demirel University) in Almaty is a prominent institution known for its strong emphasis on technology, innovation, and research. The university offers a wide range of programs, including engineering, computer science, business, and social sciences. With a modern campus and state-of-the-art facilities, SDU fosters an engaging learning environment where students can develop practical skills and theoretical knowledge. The university is also known for its multicultural community and international collaborations, promoting global perspectives.
